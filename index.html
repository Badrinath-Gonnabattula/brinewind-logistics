About Us

Brine Wind Logistics is a Highly professional, Independent and Full-service Freight Forwarder, providing all the Cargo handling facilities to our Clients under ONE ROOF that includes Custom Clearance, Freight Forwarding, Warehousing, Transportation. Brine Wind Logistics is a Newly Start-Up in SEA and AIR process. We provide all Logistics Solutions. We are based from Jaipur (Rajasthan) also known as Pink City. We have our Youngsters Team including for Sales, Marketing, Customer Support and All.
